Balsamic Baked Chicken Breast with Mozzarella Cheese

Description

A quick, one pan recipe. Tender chicken with melted mozzerella with a side of tomatoes and onions.

The balsamic shines through as the heros of the recipe and perfectly enhances the taste fo the veggies.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of italian herbs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 6 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups cheery tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup of shredded mozzerella
  • black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh chopped basil, to garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 430F
  2. Place chicken breasts in a baking dish and drizzle with olive oil. Season with oregano, basil, salt, pepper and 4 cloves of minced garlic.
  3. Arrange the tomatoes and red onion around the chicken in the dish.
  4. Whisk together the balsamic vinegar, sugar and remaining garlic in a jug to combine.
  5. Pour mix over the chicken breasts, flip each breast in the sauce to evenly coat.
  6. Bake in preheated oven for 20-25 minutes.
  7. Sprinkle with cheese and broil (or grill) for 4-5 minutes, or until cheese is melted and golden.
  8. Garnish with basil and serve with pan juices.
